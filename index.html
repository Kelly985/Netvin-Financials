Comprehensive Financial Solutions

Professional Bookkeeping

Keep your finances in perfect order with meticulous bookkeeping. We track every transaction, reconcile accounts, and ensure your records are always accurate and audit-ready.

Expert Tax Services

Navigate KRA compliance with confidence. We handle income tax, VAT, and annual returns — minimising your tax burden legally while keeping your business fully compliant.

Financial Consulting

Get strategic guidance to improve cash flow, boost profitability, and make smarter business decisions. We translate your numbers into a clear roadmap for growth.

Accounting Services

From ledger maintenance to full financial statement preparation, our accounting services give you a complete and accurate picture of your business's financial health.

Payroll Management

We handle end-to-end payroll processing including NHIF, NSSF, and PAYE deductions — ensuring your staff are paid accurately and on time, every time.

Business Advisory
